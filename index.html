Meet Amara

With over 8 years of experience in the art of henna, I've dedicated my life to preserving this ancient tradition while adding my own contemporary touch. My journey began in my grandmother's kitchen, learning the sacred recipes and techniques passed down through generations.

Today, I specialize in creating personalized henna experiences that honor both traditional artistry and modern aesthetics. Each design is carefully crafted to reflect your unique story and style.

I believe that henna is more than just body art – it's a celebration of culture, beauty, and personal expression. Let me help you create something truly special.
